# Glossary

Quick definitions for the terms used across the dashboard and these articles.

## Moving averages

- **SMA20** — the 20-period simple moving average (the "fast" MA).
- **SMA200** — the 200-period simple moving average (the "slow" MA).
- **MA cluster** — the band spanned by the fast and slow MAs together. "Above/below the cluster" means clearly outside both.
- **MA distance (ATR)** — the gap between the two MAs measured in ATR units. Drives the state.
- **MA distance (%)** — the same gap as a percent. The state qualifies on whichever of ATR or % crosses the threshold.
- **Slope** — the rate of change of an MA, in ATR. "Flat enough" for NARROW means `fast_slope ≤ 0.12 ATR` and `slow_slope ≤ 0.05 ATR`.

## Volatility and price

- **ATR** — Average True Range, a volatility unit. Distances in ATR are comparable across symbols of different price levels.
- **Gap** — `(current close − previous RTH close) / previous RTH close × 100` for `gap_pct`; the same move in ATR units for `gap_atr`.
- **Price location** — where the close sits vs the cluster: `above_cluster`, `below_cluster`, `inside_cluster`, or `near_cluster` (within `0.35 × ATR`).

## The states

- **NARROW** — MA gap in `[0.05, 0.75] ATR` or `[0.01%, 0.35%]`. `state_score` 82–94. The primary coil.
- **WIDE SNAPBACK** — gap `≥ 1.50 ATR` and price `≥ 1.0 ATR` off the fast MA. `state_score` 60. Watch reversal to 20/200.
- **TRENDING** — gap `≥ 1.50 ATR` but price within `1.0 ATR` of the fast MA. `state_score` 50. Events near 20/200 only.
- **WATCH LOOSE** — soft-tight: gap `≤ 1.25 ATR` or `≤ 0.55%` but not NARROW. `state_score` 45.
- **TOO TIGHT** — gap `< 0.05 ATR` and `< 0.01%`. `state_score` 30.
- **MIDDLE** — everything else. `state_score` 20.

## Prior45 and zones

- **Prior45** — the reference band from the previous RTH session's final 45 minutes plus the 20/200 cluster.
- **Zone height** — the larger of the cluster height or `0.25 ATR`.
- **Zone bucket** — `#1` within one zone-height of the edge, `#2` 1–2×, `#3` 2×+ (stretched).

## Score and watch labels

- **Score** — `clamp(0, 100, state_score + liquidity_score + location_score)`. A composite ranking, not a probability.
- **Liquidity score** — `min(20, log10(avg_dollar_volume) × 2)`.
- **Location score** — `+10` for above/below cluster, `+5` otherwise.
- **Watch labels** — `LONG_IF_OPEN_ABOVE_CLUSTER`, `SHORT_IF_OPEN_BELOW_CLUSTER`, `WAIT_FOR_OPEN_LOCATION`, the WIDE SNAPBACK reversal labels, `TREND_EVENT_NEAR_20`, and `NO_TRADE_YET`.
