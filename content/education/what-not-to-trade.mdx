# What not to trade

<Callout tone="caution">
Draft — pending trader review. These exclusions follow from the scanner's state and zone logic; confirm the discretionary nuances with the desk.
</Callout>

As much of the edge is in what you skip as in what you take. The scanner surfaces ~500 names a day; most of them are not trades. Here's what to pass on.

## TOO TIGHT — no expansion yet

A TOO TIGHT name has its MA gap `< 0.05 ATR` and `< 0.01%` — the moving averages are essentially stacked. There is no expansion structure to trade. `state_score` is 30 for a reason. Leave it; it may compress into something, but right now there's nothing to lean on.

## MIDDLE — undefined structure

A MIDDLE name is "not narrow or wide enough" — `state_score` 20. It isn't coiled and it isn't stretched. It's on the dashboard because liquidity or location nudged its score, not because the structure is tradeable. Treat MIDDLE as information, not a setup.

## Zone #3 — reversal-only, not continuation

When a name is in a **#3 zone** (2×+ stretched), the action label is explicit: `HIGH_3_SHORT_REVERSAL_ONLY` or `LOW_3_LONG_REVERSAL_ONLY`. Do **not** take continuation here. The higher-odds trade at an extreme is back toward the band, never further into the stretch.

## Stretched names you'd be chasing late

A WIDE SNAPBACK name is `≥ 1.50 ATR` apart and `≥ 1.0 ATR` off the fast MA — its note is "watch reversal back to 20/200," not "chase the move." Entering in the direction of an already-stretched name is the classic late-entry mistake; the further from the MA, the worse the continuation R:R.

## NARROW names that already expanded

The single most common miss: a **NARROW** name that already fired its expansion **before you saw it**. The setup was real pre-open, but if the first 2-minute candle has already broken and run, the entry and stop are no longer where the playbook places them. If the opening trigger has passed, the trade has passed — wait for the next clean setup.

<Callout tone="danger">
The opening playbook only works when you enter on the **first** 2-minute candle's structure. A NARROW name that already expanded, a #3 continuation, or a chase into a stretched WIDE SNAPBACK all break that structure. When in doubt, pass.
</Callout>
