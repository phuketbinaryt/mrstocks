# The Prior45 zone system

The **Prior45 zone band** maps where price sits relative to a reference band, and that placement changes what the same state *means*. Same NARROW setup, different zone, different play.

## How the band is anchored

The band is anchored to the **previous RTH session's final 45 minutes** combined with the current 20/200 SMA cluster. The cluster low/high spans that late-session low and high, the previous close, and both moving averages. The **zone height** is the larger of the cluster height or `0.25 ATR`, so the buckets never collapse to zero on a quiet day.

## Side and bucket

Each symbol gets a **side** and a **bucket**:

- **side** — `upper` (price above the band), `inside` (within it), or `lower` (below it).
- **bucket** — `#1` if within one zone-height of the band edge, `#2` if 1–2× away, `#3` if 2×+ (stretched).

Together they form a position label like `upper #1` or `lower #2`. Inside has no bucket.

## Reading the actions

The pattern is symmetric. **Inside** waits for a break. **#1** allows continuation in the zone's direction. **#2** says caution — wait for an event. **#3** flips you to reversal-only: at an extreme, the higher-odds trade is back toward the band, not further away from it. Pair the zone with the state and watch direction to decide whether a setup is worth your attention at the open.
