# The six setup states

Every candidate carries exactly one **setup state**. The state captures where price sits relative to its 20- and 200-day moving averages, measured in ATR. Read the state first — it tells you which mental model to apply before you open the chart. There are six, and only six.

<StateBadgeRow />

## NARROW

The MA gap is in `[0.05, 0.75] ATR` **or** `[0.01%, 0.35%]`. This is the primary setup — the 20 and 200 are coiled tight, often before a directional expansion. Its `state_score` runs **82–94**: highest in the middle of the tight band, decaying toward the edges. If the MAs are still sloping (not "flat enough" — `fast_slope ≤ 0.12 ATR` and `slow_slope ≤ 0.05 ATR`), it loses 8 points and gets the note "MAs tight but still sloping."

**Play:** wait for the opening trigger to confirm direction (see the playbook). **Pitfall:** a NARROW name that already expanded before you saw it.

## WIDE SNAPBACK

The MA gap is `≥ 1.50 ATR` **and** price is `≥ 1.0 ATR` from the fast MA. `state_score` is **60** — the second-best state. The scanner's own note: "watch reversal back to 20/200." The thesis is a counter-move back toward the cluster, not a fresh trend trade.

**Play:** watch for the reversal back to 20/200. **Pitfall:** in a strong trend the stretch can persist.

## TRENDING

The MA gap is `≥ 1.50 ATR` but price is **within** `1.0 ATR` of the fast MA. `state_score` is **50**. Note: "only events near 20/200." The cluster is wide, but price is riding the fast MA — treat it as a trend, only acting on events right at the 20/200.

**Play:** only events near the 20/200. **Pitfall:** chasing far from the MA.

## WATCH LOOSE

Soft-tight: the gap is `≤ 1.25 ATR` **or** `≤ 0.55%` but doesn't qualify as NARROW. `state_score` is **45**. When the MAs aren't flat, the note reads "MAs close but not flat."

**Play:** keep it on watch — it may compress into NARROW. **Pitfall:** treating it as a NARROW signal early.

## TOO TIGHT

The MA gap is `< 0.05 ATR` **and** `< 0.01%`. `state_score` is **30**. Note: "MA distance below tight minimum." The MAs are essentially on top of each other — there is no expansion structure to trade yet.

**Play:** none — wait. **Pitfall:** mistaking dead-flat for coiled.

## MIDDLE

Everything else — not narrow enough, not wide enough. `state_score` is **20**. Note: "not narrow or wide enough." These are unremarkable; they surface because some combination of liquidity or location nudged their score.

**Play:** watch-list material at best. **Pitfall:** forcing a trade on an undefined structure.

## How they combine

The state alone isn't the whole picture. Pair it with the **Prior45 zone** and the **score** — a NARROW name above the cluster with a high score is a very different read from a MIDDLE name. The next articles cover both.
