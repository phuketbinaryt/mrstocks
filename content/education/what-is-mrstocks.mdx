# What MR/STOCKS is

MR/STOCKS is a **pre-open stock scanner**. It runs once each morning, reads the previous regular-session picture for a large universe of US equities, and hands you a ranked shortlist of names whose 20- and 200-day moving averages are coiled, stretched, or trending in a tradeable way.

## The 09:15 daily flow

Around **09:15 NY**, before the regular session opens, the scanner does its work:

1. It **freezes the previous RTH session's state** — it does not peek at today's open. Everything you see is the picture *going into* the day.
2. It classifies every symbol into one of **six setup states** (NARROW, WIDE SNAPBACK, TRENDING, WATCH LOOSE, TOO TIGHT, MIDDLE).
3. It scores and ranks them — roughly **500 candidates** land on the dashboard, sorted so the strongest setups rise to the top.

By the time you open the dashboard, the list is already there. It does not change intraday — it is a pre-market read, then you take it to your charts.

<Callout tone="info">
The scanner is **advisory**. It surfaces the pre-open picture and a conditional bias. The actual entry always needs the opening event to confirm — see the opening playbook.
</Callout>

## What a member does with it

- **Scan the dashboard** before the open and filter by state, Prior45 zone, or one of your watchlists.
- **Find the A+ setups** — typically NARROW names with a clean location and a high score.
- **Wait for the opening trigger** — the first regular-session 2-minute candle decides direction (covered in the Strategy tier).
- **Save names to watchlists** and **set alerts** so tomorrow's scan pings you when a matching setup appears.

## What it does not do

- **Not real-time.** It does not stream intraday quotes.
- **Not a buy signal.** A candidate is a *setup* with a *conditional* bias, not an order.
- **Not advice.** Trade your own book; read the terms.
